Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Rescue Rush is playable without creating an account. We collect limited product analytics to understand game performance, level difficulty, and reward-ad interest.

Information We Store

  • Local game stats such as runs, wins, revives, shares, selected country, and selected theme.
  • Anonymous analytics events such as page views, level starts, level failures, tool use, and ad offers.
  • Basic technical information provided by the browser, such as page path and referrer.

Local Storage

The game uses browser local storage to keep lightweight gameplay metrics and test analytics on your device. Clearing browser data removes these local records.

Third-Party Services

We use PostHog for product analytics. If real advertising is added later, ad partners may process data needed to deliver, measure, and prevent fraud for ads.

Contact

Questions can be sent through the contact page.
