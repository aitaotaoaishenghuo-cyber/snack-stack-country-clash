Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Rescue Rush: Country Clash is playable without creating an account. This policy explains what information is processed when you visit the site, play the game, use optional reward features, or interact with analytics and advertising services.

Information We Process

  • Local game stats such as runs, wins, revives, shares, selected country, and selected theme.
  • Gameplay events such as page views, level starts, level failures, completions, tool use, and ad offers.
  • Basic technical information such as page path, referrer, browser information, approximate device type, and timestamps.
  • Advertising and consent signals required to show, measure, and control ads where applicable.

Local Storage

The game uses browser local storage to keep lightweight gameplay metrics and test analytics on your device. Clearing browser data removes these local records.

Cookies And Consent

Advertising, analytics, and consent-management services may use cookies or similar technologies. For users in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, the site uses Google's consent management platform to request and store advertising choices where required.

Third-Party Services

We use PostHog to understand product behavior and improve the game. We use Google AdSense code for site verification and future advertising review. If real ads are enabled, Google and other approved ad partners may process data needed to deliver ads, measure performance, limit fraud, and respect consent choices.

Rewarded Ads

Rewarded ad placements are optional. If a rewarded ad is available, players may choose to view it for an in-game benefit such as a revive or tool. The reward is game-only and has no cash value.

Children

Rescue Rush is not directed specifically to children. We do not knowingly ask for names, addresses, or account registration from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the contact channel, please contact us so we can review and remove it where appropriate.

Your Choices

You can clear local storage and cookies through your browser settings. In regions where a consent message is shown, you can manage advertising consent through that message.
